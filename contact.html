Frequently Asked

A few things that come up.

Our sizes run true to most premium Canadian womenswear. If you're between sizes, size up for a relaxed fit or down for tailored. Our fit guide is detailed on each product page, and we offer free exchanges if it's not right.
Orders ship from Hamilton within 1–2 business days. Most Ontario addresses receive orders in 2–4 business days; rest of Canada in 3–7 days. Hamilton-area customers can choose studio pickup.
Free returns within 30 days on unworn, unwashed pieces with original tags. Sale items are final sale. Gently used returns are inspected and donated to Hamilton-area nursing programs.
Yes — we work with healthcare employers, clinic groups, and uniform programs. Reach out to trade@roshnithelabel.com with your organization size and needs.
Machine wash cold with like colours. Tumble dry low. Do not bleach. Do not iron. Wash inside out to protect the colour. Our fabric is engineered for 100+ industrial washes without pilling.
