Our Story

A Canadian brand, with honest origins.

Roshni means light in Bengali. The name is personal — our founder is Bangladeshi-Canadian, and this label was built to do what so many brands won't: name the factory, pay above market, and make the origin a point of pride.

We design in Hamilton, Ontario. We make in a certified, Accord-audited factory in Dhaka. And we sell to healthcare professionals across Canada who've learned to expect more from a twelve-hour shift.

The Fabric

Engineered for a 12-hour shift.

Our signature fabric is a 215 GSM four-way-stretch performance knit. Quick-drying. Fluid-resistant, PFC-free. Finished with an anti-pilling wash so it looks like new through a hundred-plus cycles.

Cut from recycled polyester, rayon, and a touch of spandex. OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified. Woven at a mill you can name.
