Our Story

A Canadian label, with honest origins.

Roshni means light in Bengali. The name is personal. The work is not.

The Founder

From Dhaka to Hamilton.

Our founder, Rubaiyat Jahan, moved to Canada from Bangladesh as a teenager. Years later, watching nurses on her aunt's ward work a twelve-hour shift in scrubs that fit badly and looked cheaper than the work deserved, she saw a gap — and a way to put her two homes into one label.

Roshni is a Canadian brand. It's also a quiet love letter to the country that taught her how to make clothes well, and the country that gave her the chance to build a company.

The Idea

Why start a scrub brand?

Because healthcare workers, the ones we trust with the hardest hours of our lives, deserve clothes that were designed for them. Not hand-me-down uniforms. Not polyester boxes sewn at the lowest possible margin.

We looked at what was available in Canada and saw two choices: commodity scrubs from uniform warehouses, or premium US brands that have never set foot in this country. Nothing in between. Nothing Canadian. Nothing made with real transparency.

"We want to name our factory. We want you to know who made your scrubs. That's the difference."

So we started something small. One tech pack. One factory. Four pieces. A founder, her day job, and an unreasonable standard for what a scrub should feel like at hour eleven.

Built from scratch, for real shifts.

Every piece in the Launch Capsule was sampled a minimum of three times before we placed an order. Every sample was worn by a healthcare professional in Hamilton for at least a week. Every seam you see was put there for a reason — a pen pocket where you actually reach for one, a vent where your hip actually bends, a drawstring that holds when you run.

Designed in Hamilton, Ontario.

Hamilton is an old manufacturing city. Steeltown. The place where Canadians have been making things, well, for a hundred and fifty years. We're proud to add a line to that history.

What We Stand For

Three things we do differently.

We name the factory

Our factory is in Gazipur, just outside Dhaka. Accord-certified. BSCI-audited. On our product pages, on our packaging, in our About.

We wear-test everything

No piece goes to production until a real healthcare worker has worn it for a full shift and given us feedback we've acted on.

We pay above market

We pay our factory a premium over the local market rate. It's documented. It's published. It's a line item we will never move on.
